ABOUT



Welcome! My name is Rachel and you've reached my portfolio of personal projects in the areas of UX/UI and Logo Design. My first project here was the Hourglass website design prototype, the first version of which I started work on in December 2021. Since then, this portfolio has served as a journey of self-teaching and diligent, gradually improved UI design, UX research, and logo design. Adobe Creative Cloud applications XD and Illustrator, and the digital illustration app Procreate, are the primary design tools I used in building these projects. Maze is the user testing website I implemented to gather real user insights on the first versions of my UI projects, which helped guide me in creating my finalized versions.

I am open to entry-level, remote design roles within teams interested in welcoming first-time designers. In my spare time, I love working out, volunteering, traveling, video games, and watching movies and TV.
